**Now it‚Äôs time to set the metadata for your videos**

6. To get started click the edit button on the video you want to edit metadata for

<figure><img src="/images/IMG_5818.JPG" alt="" /><figcaption><p>Edit Video Metadata</p></figcaption></figure>

At any point you can hit the 'Save' button to save your progress.

<figure><img src="/images/IMG_5821.JPG" alt="" /><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Set the title of your video and add a summary including any links
* Upload a thumbnail image that will be displayed everywhere your video is shown on Rad TV
  * This image needs to be 1920x1080 (16:9)
    * JPG, PNG, GIF, or SVG
  * Max file size is 5MB
  * If your video is featured in our main carousel you might want to update your thumbnail image using this [template](https://drive.google.com/file/d/13diKPEeNVvr2b4r_MhlH1TnN5C4B_3Gh/view?usp=sharing).
* Set Video Visibility

  * If you want your video to be publicly available click the 'Video Visibility' dropdown box and select 'Public'

<figure><img src="/images/IMG_5823.JPG" alt="" /><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Choose Video Categories: Select a few categories your video belongs in. This gives your video more visibility and better discoverability across Rad TV apps.

<figure><img src="/images/IMG_5825.JPG" alt="" /><figcaption><p>Category Selection</p></figcaption></figure>

Rad TV supports NSFW content as long as the content is appropriately tagged NSFW. You can mark content as NSFW by selecting 'NSFW' from the Video Rating dropdown menu pictured below.

<figure><img src="/images/IMG_5829.JPG" alt="" /><figcaption><p>Video Rating</p></figcaption></figure>

Rad TV supports many resolutions and content types. If you are uploading VR, 360¬∞, 180¬∞, or 3D content you will need to select a 'Video Hint' in order to tell our system how to display the video. You can change this anytime. If you aren't sure which to select, trial and error will only take a few clicks.&#x20;

<figure><img src="/images/IMG_5831.JPG" alt="" /><figcaption><p>Video Hint</p></figcaption></figure>
