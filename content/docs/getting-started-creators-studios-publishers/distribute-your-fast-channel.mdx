# Distribute Your FAST Channel

**Rad TV has 45+ FAST channels live today with some of the best FAST channel partners in the world.**&#x20;

If you're a content creator, studio, or content owner that has an existing FAST channel, you can can get started for free at RadTV.com and instantly have your channels live across all of Rad TV's apps.

Watch this [How-To Video](https://rad.live/watch/feature/06e1d6f2-28db-461f-9dc4-df8eff985541/).

## Getting Started

To get started you will need a free account and a creators channel at [RadTV.com](https://radtv.com).&#x20;

Once you have your free account click the 'Create' button at the top of the page to create your channel.

<figure><img src="/images/IMG_5641.JPG" alt="" /><figcaption><p>Click 'Create'</p></figcaption></figure>

The Creator Channel you are creating now will serve as your top level branding across the Rad TV ecosystem. This branded channel will be home to all of the content and Live TV Channels you distribute through Rad TV. Of course you can create many channels, but each login can only host 1 channel. Often times, creators have several brands that they work under. Feel free to create as many channels as you need.

Once you are finished with the Channel creation process you will be able to access the Live TV section within your [Creator Studio](https://rad.live/channel/).

<figure><img src="/images/Screenshot-2025-05-28-at-3.50.50-PM.png" alt="" /><figcaption><p>Creator Dashboard</p></figcaption></figure>

You can navigate to your Creator Studio anytime by clicking on your profile pic in the top right hand corner and then selecting Creator Studio as pictured below

<figure><img src="/images/Click-Creator-Studio.JPG" alt="" /><figcaption><p>Select Creator Studio</p></figcaption></figure>

Once you are in your Creator Studio you will see Live TV in the left navigation as pictured below

<figure><img src="/images/Click-Live-TV.JPG" alt="" /><figcaption><p>Click 'Live TV'</p></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="/images/Live-TV-Channels.png" alt="" /><figcaption><p>Live TV</p></figcaption></figure>

Click 'Create Your First Channel' or '+ Create Channel' to add your FAST channel.

<figure><img src="/images/Create-New-Live-TV-Channel.png" alt="" /><figcaption><p>Create New Channel Form</p></figcaption></figure>

This form allows you to enter your metadata and Stream URL. Once your information looks good and you have uploaded a Channel Thumbnail, click 'Create Channel' at the bottom of the form. You will see your channel creation process is initiated.&#x20;
