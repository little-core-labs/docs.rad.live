# Sell Individual Videos

**Monetizing Video Content**

You can publish video content utilizing Ethereum based smart contracts and Coinbase's Layer 2 network, Base, with support for other chains coming soon.&#x20;



You can sell:

* Open Editions: Unlimited copies available at a set price
* Limited Editions: A limited number of copies available at a set price
* Timed Open Editions: Unlimited copies available for a limited time

**Prepare Your Content:**

* Rad TV supports a wide range of video file types including WEBM, MP4, MOV, MKV, AVI, and MXF.
* Video files must be less than 100GB in size, and max 4K (3840 x 2160) resolution.
* Thumbnails must be 1920x1080 or 16:9 ratio, no larger than 4MB, and JPG, PNG, or JPEG format.

You could have 1 contract that holds everything you create or you could deploy a new collection each time and organize your pieces into different baskets/collections.  Let's say you are making a series of pieces that all have a similar style. You most likely would want those pieces to live together inside of the same collection so that they are easily discoverable and the narrative around the pieces is the same.&#x20;
