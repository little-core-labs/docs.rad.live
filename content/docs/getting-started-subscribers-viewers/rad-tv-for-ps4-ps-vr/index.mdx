# Rad TV for PS4 / PS VR

## How to Install

Search "Rad TV" on the PlayStation store. Available for PS4 and PS5 consoles, PS VR, and PS VR2.

<Callout type="info">
Sony Interactive Entertainment removed streaming apps from its web-based store, so you need to go onto the device itself and find the application inside the store application, NOT on the website. Only games are listed on the PlayStation website.
</Callout>

## How to View RSS Feeds

1. Add RSS feeds to your Rad TV account using the instructions [here](/docs/../adding-external-rss-feeds-to-your-library#viewing-feeds-on-rad-tv).
2. Open the Rad TV app.
3. Make sure you are logged in and have a [Rad TV Premium membership](/docs/../premium-and-free-on-demand-video-and-free-tv).
4. Go to your Library and open the RSS Feeds section.
5. If the feed you added on your Rad TV account parsed correctly it will show up as a folder (or folder thumbnail).

<Callout type="info">
If the feed is listed on your [RSS Feeds page](https://rad.live/editaccount/?activeTab=rssfeeds) but does not show up in the application, it's likely that the application failed to parse the feed. Please check to make sure the feed follows our standards.
</Callout>
